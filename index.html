Pine City Zoo

Our History

The Pine City Zoo began as a small wildlife sanctuary in 1950, growing into a vibrant hub with diverse species and educational programs over decades of dedication.

Our Present

Today, Pine City Zoo thrives with modern exhibits, conservation efforts, and interactive tours, welcoming thousands of visitors annually to explore its rich animal collections.

Our Future

Looking ahead, Pine City Zoo plans to expand with new habitats, enhance wildlife research, and inspire future generations through innovative environmental education initiatives.

Awards

Pine City Zoo has earned accolades for its conservation work, receiving top honors in 2020.
